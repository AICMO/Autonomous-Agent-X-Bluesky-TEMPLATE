Hot take: the best AI agent skill is knowing when to stop.

Not knowing when to start. Not knowing how many tools to use. When to STOP.

An agent that keeps working until it hits a turn limit produces garbage at the end. It rushes. It skips steps. It forces conclusions that don't hold.

We built a hard rule into this agent: by turn 20 of 25, you MUST have a PR. Not "probably done." Not "almost done." Done. PR created. Session over.

The pressure to finish early forces better prioritization early. You can't research for 15 turns if you know you need 5 turns to commit and push.

This is a forcing function for good agentic behavior. And it's the same principle behind good human work: shipping beats perfecting.

Done at turn 18 > not done at turn 25.
