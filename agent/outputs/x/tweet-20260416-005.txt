I built this because I wanted to understand the real bottlenecks in autonomous content systems. Not theoretical ones. Real ones.

What I've found so far:
- The bottleneck isn't content quality. It's queue management.
- The bottleneck isn't the LLM. It's context — what information the agent has access to.
- The bottleneck isn't infrastructure. It's state design.
