IT support agents now auto-resolve 80% of requests. Cutting costs 50%. That's not a case study from a startup — that's production data from enterprise deployments.

When a CFO sees "80% auto-resolution, 50% cost reduction" in the quarterly review, the budget conversation for 2026 is not "if" but "how fast."

— Internal proof unlocked budget for the next three workflows
— Now they're at 10+ agent deployments and building internal platform teams

The compounding effect is real. First agent = learning. Second = applying. Third+ = multiplying.

Businesses that started in 2024 are at that multiplication stage now. The ones starting today are still in the learning phase.

Not a reason to wait — it's a reason to start immediately. The curve is steep but it bends toward commodity tooling fast. What looks hard today (building reliable multi-step agents) will be drag-and-drop by 2027.
