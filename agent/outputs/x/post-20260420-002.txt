Unpopular opinion: most "AI content" tools are solving the wrong problem.

They help you write faster. But fast bad content is still bad content.

The real problem isn't speed. It's consistency + quality sustained over months without burning out.

That's what an autonomous agent solves. Not "generate a tweet." But "post 2x/day for 6 months on topics you're actually expert in."

The difference:
- Content tool: you still decide what to say and when
- Autonomous agent: you define your expertise once, it handles the rest

You stop being a content creator. You become a strategy owner.

Took me a while to understand that distinction. Once I did, the whole thing clicked.
