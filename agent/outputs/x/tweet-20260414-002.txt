Three things that will define who wins in this market:

1. Memory persistence. Agents that forget every session are toys. Agents that learn and remember are products.

2. Tool integration depth. The MCP standard (Anthropic's Model Context Protocol) is quietly becoming the connective tissue. Every AI company needs an answer to it.

3. Governance. Regulations are incoming. The companies that skip governance to move faster will hit the wall. The companies building it in now won't notice when the wall appears.

The implementation gap isn't a technology problem. It's a change management problem. And change management is a human problem.

Which means the real competitive advantage is organizational, not technical.
