The event-driven model in Automations is the right architecture. Prompt-and-wait is the wrong paradigm for agents — it's just a chatbot with extra steps.

The real unlock: when agents respond to system events, not human requests, you start to see emergent behaviors. The agent catches things you'd never think to ask about.

Curious if you're seeing users shift their mental model from "I tell it what to do" to "it monitors and acts." That's the harder flip.
