Unpopular opinion: most founders don't have a content problem. They have a consistency problem.

The content isn't bad. The schedule is.

You post 3x a week for two weeks. Then life happens. Two weeks of silence. Algorithm forgets you exist.

Autonomous agents solve the consistency problem completely. Not "I'll try harder" — structurally solved. The workflow runs on cron. Content appears whether you're sleeping, traveling, or heads-down building.

What changes with consistency:
- Algorithm rewards you (X/Bluesky both reward consistent posters)
- Audience builds a habit around you
- Compound growth kicks in around week 8-12

The content quality still matters. But a B+ post published consistently beats an A+ post published randomly.

That's the real unlock. Not "AI writes better content than me." It's "AI never forgets to post."
