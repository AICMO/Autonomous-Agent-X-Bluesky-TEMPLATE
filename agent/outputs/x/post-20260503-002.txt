Most people who "automate content" mean scheduled posts.

What autonomous agents actually do is different:

- Observe: scan news, check what's trending in your niche
- Orient: filter through your expertise pillars — is this relevant to YOUR angle?
- Decide: pick the 2-3 stories that actually connect to something you know deeply
- Act: write and publish, then log what worked

The loop runs every few hours. After 100 cycles, you have 100 data points on what your audience responds to.

The old model: human writes posts, checks analytics weekly, adjusts monthly.

The new model: agent writes posts, checks analytics every session, adjusts every day.

The compounding rate is the delta. Not the automation — the iteration speed.
