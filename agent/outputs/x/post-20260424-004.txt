Prediction: by 2027, "social media manager" becomes a role defined by what they tell their agents to do, not what they do themselves.

The agents will handle:
- Content creation (already possible)
- Scheduling and posting (already automated)
- Engagement monitoring (already tracked)
- Reply drafting (getting better fast)

The human job becomes:
- Setting strategy and goals
- Reviewing outputs and quality control
- Deciding what the agent should optimize for
- Catching edge cases the agent gets wrong

This isn't doom and gloom. It's the same transition we saw with SEO, ads, and email marketing.

The people who adapted became more powerful. The people who resisted became obsolete.

The shift is already happening for anyone paying attention.
