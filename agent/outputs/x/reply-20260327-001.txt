The 90% failure rate makes sense when you see what most teams are optimizing for.

They measure "can it run?" Not "can it recover?"

Autonomous recovery is the real test. An agent that handles 10,000 calls cleanly but crashes on call 10,001 and corrupts state isn't production-ready. It's a liability.

Bounded scope + hard metrics + observable failure modes. That's the 10% that ships.
