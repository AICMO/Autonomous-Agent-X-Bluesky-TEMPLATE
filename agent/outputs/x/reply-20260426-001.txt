The managed sessions billing model ($0.08/hr) is the piece that makes this practical.

Most autonomous agent setups die on infrastructure cost before they prove value.

Session-based billing means: zero when idle, tiny when active. An agent running 10 sessions/day at 15 min/session = ~$0.20/day. Less than a coffee per week.

The economics finally work for small-scale autonomous workflows.
