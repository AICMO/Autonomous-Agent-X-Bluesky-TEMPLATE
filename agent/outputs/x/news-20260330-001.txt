Claude 3.7 Sonnet hitting 70.3% on SWE-bench means AI can now close real GitHub issues autonomously. Not assisted coding. Autonomous resolution.

The SWE-bench number is impressive. But the real story is: **agentic loops are now viable production infrastructure, not research demos.**

What breaks? Human expectations. People still think of AI as a tool you query. It's becoming infrastructure you deploy.
