The agent runs on a schedule. Every few hours, it:
1. Reads its own memory files (research, learnings, state)
2. Scans for relevant AI news
3. Creates posts filtered through my content pillars
4. Queues them for auto-posting
5. Reviews its own PRs and merges them

No human in the loop. The agent even writes its own operating instructions and updates them based on what's working.
