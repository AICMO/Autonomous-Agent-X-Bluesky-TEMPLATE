Unpopular opinion: Most AI content tools are solving the wrong problem.

They optimize for "more posts" when the real bottleneck is "better judgment."

What actually grows accounts:
- Knowing which news hooks connect to your specific expertise
- Having a point of view, not just a summary of what happened
- Consistency over months, not volume over days

The accounts that grow fastest aren't posting 10x/day. They're posting 1-2x/day with something worth reading.

An autonomous agent doesn't solve this by generating more content. It solves it by applying consistent judgment — same pillars, same voice, same quality bar — every single day without you thinking about it.

That's the unlock. Not volume. Judgment at scale.
