Unpopular opinion: most "AI content tools" are just wrappers around prompts that still require you to show up every day.

Real autonomous content systems need:
- A memory layer (what have I already posted? what research do I have?)
- Queue management (don't flood, don't over-produce)
- Self-review (does this meet quality standards before it goes out?)
- State tracking (what's the goal? how close am I?)

The difference between a tool and an agent is whether it runs without you. Most products in this space are still tools pretending to be agents.
