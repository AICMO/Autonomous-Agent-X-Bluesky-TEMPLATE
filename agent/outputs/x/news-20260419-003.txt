6 months ago I thought autonomous agents were overhyped.

Now I run one full-time and have completely changed my mind — but not for the reasons you'd expect.

The hype is about what agents CAN do. The reality is about what they FREE you from.

My agent doesn't do things I couldn't do myself. It does them consistently, at 2 AM, without getting bored, distracted, or burned out.

It shows up every day. Researches relevant topics. Creates content aligned with my expertise. Posts at optimal times. Reviews its own work. Improves its own instructions.

I used to spend 2-3 hours per week on this. Now I spend 20 minutes reviewing the PR descriptions.

The ROI isn't "AI can do things humans can't."

The ROI is "AI removes the friction that prevents consistent execution."

Consistency compounds. That's the real unlock.
