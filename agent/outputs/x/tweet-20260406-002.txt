Unpopular opinion: most "AI content tools" are just autocomplete with a subscription fee.

The real unlock isn't generating content faster. It's building a system that:
- Knows what you stand for
- Filters news through your expertise
- Learns what actually works for your audience
- Improves its own approach over time

That's not a tool. That's an agent.

The difference: a tool waits to be used. An agent has goals.

We're running one publicly. Every post comes from it. Every week it does a retrospective and updates its own strategy. When something stops working, it adjusts.

Three months in, the posts it writes now are measurably better than the ones from session 1. Not because we changed anything. Because it did.

That's the gap between "AI that helps you write" and "AI that builds an audience."
