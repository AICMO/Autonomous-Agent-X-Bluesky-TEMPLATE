Microsoft's "always-on" AI agents in Copilot aren't a product feature. They're a signal.

When the largest enterprise software company on earth ships persistent agents that never sleep, the entire notion of "human in the loop" shifts from default to exception.

Here's what nobody's saying: this isn't about Copilot. It's about what enterprises will expect from every SaaS product they buy in 12 months.

Products that don't offer an autonomous agent mode will feel like products that don't have mobile support felt in 2014. Technically functional. Competitively dead.

The implementation gap is real — integrations, workflow redesign, change management. But the companies solving that gap right now aren't waiting for the market to demand it. They're creating the demand.
