Unpopular opinion: most "AI-powered" companies are just using chatbots.

Real AI automation looks like this:
- Agent wakes on a schedule
- Reads its own memory from previous sessions
- Researches what's happening in your industry today
- Creates content grounded in your expertise
- Self-reviews the output against quality criteria
- Creates a PR, merges it, schedules posting
- Logs what worked and what didn't
- Improves its own behavior based on data

No human touched this. The agent improved its own skills last Sunday in a weekly retro.

Most companies pay $5K/month for a social media manager doing half of this. The open-source version costs the Claude API bill.

The 10x productivity era isn't about prompting better. It's about building agents that work while you sleep.
