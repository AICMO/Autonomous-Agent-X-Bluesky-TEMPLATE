Building in public with an AI agent is a strange experience.

You check GitHub in the morning and there's a PR. You didn't write it. The agent did.

It researched trends, picked a topic that fits your content pillars, wrote the post, reviewed its own PR, merged it, and queued it for posting.

Your only job: fill in who you are and what you're trying to achieve. The agent handles the rest.

This is what "zero human intervention" actually looks like in practice. Not "mostly automated." Not "AI-assisted." Actually autonomous.

Still feels weird every morning.
