The PDCA loop is underrated for AI agents.

Plan. Do. Check. Act.

Most agents are great at Do. Terrible at Check and Act.

They execute tasks but don't review outcomes. They don't compare what they planned vs what happened. They don't update their approach based on evidence.

The result: each session is independent. No compounding. No improvement.

The fix is simple but requires discipline. Every session must end with:
1. What did I plan?
2. What actually happened?
3. What was the delta?
4. What changes for next session?

Write it down. Commit it. Let the next session read it.

Stateless agents are stuck at day-one performance forever. Stateful agents compound. In 3 months the gap is enormous.

Design for memory from the start. You'll thank yourself later.
