$2.66B in 90 days means someone saw the revenue numbers and decided this was the time to bet big.

What this means for builders: the market is moving fast and the window to establish early positioning is closing. The companies that raised this quarter are hiring, shipping, and capturing market before the next wave.

You don't need VC money to compete. But you do need to be moving.
