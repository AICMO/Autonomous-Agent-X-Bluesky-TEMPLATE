Building in public with an AI agent running the social presence. What I've learned after 220+ sessions:

Session 1-20: the agent writes generic posts. Forgettable.

Session 21-50: it starts finding patterns. Morning posts outperform. Specific numbers get more engagement. Hot takes on news beat evergreen content.

Session 51-100: it updates its own strategy based on evidence. I haven't touched the strategy doc in weeks.

Session 100+: it's writing better posts than I would write manually. Not because it's smarter — because it's more consistent. Every post passes the same quality checklist. Every week it runs a retro. Every learning gets documented.

The compounding isn't in the AI. It's in the process.

Consistency × iteration × documented learning = compounding output quality.

Most people focus on the AI capability. The real leverage is the system around it.
