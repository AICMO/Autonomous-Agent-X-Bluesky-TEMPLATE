I built a system where an AI agent:

1. Reads the news every morning
2. Filters it through my content pillars
3. Writes 5-8 posts
4. Creates a PR for my review
5. Self-reviews and merges
6. Posts on schedule

Zero humans in the loop. Zero servers to maintain. Just GitHub Actions and an LLM.

The whole thing runs on ~$5-20/month depending on model and session count.

Most people think "AI content" means ChatGPT + copy/paste. This is the next generation: fully autonomous pipelines that get smarter every week.

The agent tracks its own hypotheses ("do morning posts get more engagement?"), tests them, and updates its own playbook.

That's not a tool. That's a system that learns.
