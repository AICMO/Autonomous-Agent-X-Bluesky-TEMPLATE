### 1. Enterprise Adoption Hits Inflection Point
- 72% of Global 2000 now operating AI agents IN PRODUCTION (not pilots)
- 97% of executives deployed agents in the past year
- 52% of employees already using agents daily
- $600B+ invested in AI agent ecosystems in 2026
- Market: $9.14B now → $139B by 2034 (40.5% CAGR)
- Pillar: Autonomous AI agents | Angle: pilot phase is over, this is enterprise infrastructure

- 7-package multi-language system with sub-millisecond policy engine

- Targets EU AI Act, HIPAA, SOC2 standards
- Pillar: Agentic workflows | Angle: governance was the blocker — it's now solved

### 3. No-Code Agent Platform: Agentshub.AI (April 6, 2026)
- Full no-code platform for building, deploying, scaling autonomous AI workforces
- Pillar: Building with AI | Angle: skill floor dropped 10x — everyone can build agents now

### 4. Anthropic vs. OpenAI Market Share Shift
- Anthropic: 40% of enterprise LLM API spend (up from ~10% in 2024)
- OpenAI: dropped to 27% (from 50% in 2023)
- Enterprises prefer Claude for safety + governance in agentic workflows
- Pillar: Autonomous AI agents | Angle: enterprises are choosing Claude for agents

### 5. 40% of Business Apps Will Use Agents by EOY 2026
- From <5% in 2025 to 40% by end of 2026 = 8x growth in 12 months
- IT support agents now auto-resolve 80% of requests, cutting costs 50%
- 57% of orgs already deploy multi-step agent workflows
- Pillar: Agentic workflows | Angle: velocity story — 8x adoption curve is not slowing
