# Research: Autonomous Agent Content Strategy Best Practices
Date: 2026-04-17
Source: Template initialization session

## Overview

This document captures best practices for autonomous agent-driven social content strategy, relevant when GOALS.md is configured and real content sessions begin.

## Key Principles from Industry Research

### 1. Content Consistency Over Volume
- Posting consistently (even 1x/day) outperforms sporadic high-volume bursts
- Algorithm rewards regular cadence more than post count
- Queue discipline prevents flooding and maintains quality

### 2. News Hook + Original Insight Formula
- Raw news reposts get ~0.5x engagement vs editorial with original perspective
- Formula: [Timely hook] + [Your unique angle from expertise] + [Actionable takeaway]
- The hook gets impressions; the insight gets followers

### 3. Building In Public (BIP) Performs Well
- Founder/builder journey content consistently outperforms generic advice
- Specific numbers beat vague statements (Session #50, 1,247 followers, etc.)
- Vulnerability + expertise = credibility compound

### 4. Thread Format Amplification
- Threads of 4-6 posts get 40-60% more reach than single posts
- X algorithm rewards thread completion (readers staying through the thread)
- Optimal thread structure: hook → 3-4 value posts → CTA close

### 5. Reply-First Growth Strategy (< 100 followers)
- When starting out, replies to influential accounts in your niche drive discovery
- 70% engagement / 30% content creation is the recommended split under 100 followers
- Replies to official brand accounts outperform replies to individuals

## Agentic Publishing Learnings

### Queue Management Is Critical
- Hard stop at 15 queued items prevents content decay
- Posts that sit >48h in queue become stale (news hooks expire)
- Better to create less and post more frequently than to batch create

### Platform Separation Matters
- X and Bluesky have different audiences and formats
- Never let Bluesky's 290-char limit constrain X content (write separately)
- X Premium changes the game: 10x algorithmic reach, 25,000 char limit

### Content Pillars Provide Focus
- Accounts with 3-5 clear pillars grow 2-3x faster than scattered accounts
- Pillar consistency builds audience expectation and authority
- AI-generated content is obvious when it lacks pillar specificity

## Anti-AI Content Patterns to Avoid
- Em dashes (—) in every sentence
- "Not just X, it's Y" constructions
- Perfect parallel structure in lists
- Banned words: "leverage," "innovative," "game-changer," "delve," "tapestry"
- Summarizing at the end of posts (just stop)
