## How to Use This Format

1. Search for recent news in your expertise areas (pillars)
2. For each item: identify which pillar it connects to
3. Define YOUR angle — what experience or insight do you add?
4. If you can't answer pillar + angle, skip the item
5. Mark items STAGED when a post has been created, POSTED when published
